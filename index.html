Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join PulseFit?

Fill the booking form, select your membership, add classes (optional), and choose payment method. You'll get a confirmation email.

Are classes mandatory?

No, our general membership is for independent training. Classes are optional.

What payment options are available?

Pay online via Razorpay or cash at the gym.

How do I renew my membership?

Go to the Renew Membership section, enter your registered email, select the duration and payment method to extend your membership.
